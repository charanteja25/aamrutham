Our Story

Born from a boy's love
for mangoes

Ever since childhood, mangoes have been more than just a fruit to us — they were joy, they were summer, they were family. That love never faded.

"Aam + Amrutham. We believe the perfect mango tastes like pure అమృతం — divine nectar."

With Aamrutham, our goal is to bring you undiscovered, rich-tasting mango varieties you've never tasted before — alongside delightful mango-based desserts. Every fruit is sourced from our own farms in Bobbili, grown without chemicals, harvested at peak season.

Our logo tells the whole story: the boy joyfully plucking the mango from the tree — that's the founder, the pure excitement and real love for mangoes. The girl eagerly waiting — that's you, anticipating that first divine bite. This is the ఆమృతం experience we promise.
