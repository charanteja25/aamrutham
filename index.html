Our Story

The Beginning

Born from a boy's love
for mangoes

Ever since childhood, mangoes have been more than just a fruit — they were joy, they were summer, they were family. That love never faded.

"Aam + Amrutham. We believe the perfect mango tastes like pure అమృతం — divine nectar."

With Aamrutham, our goal is to bring you undiscovered, rich-tasting mango varieties you've never tasted before. Every fruit is sourced from our own farms in Bobbili, grown without chemicals, harvested only at peak season.

Our logo tells the whole story: the boy joyfully plucking the mango — that's the founder. The girl eagerly waiting — that's you, anticipating that first divine bite. This is the Aamrutham experience we promise.

Farm to Your Doorstep

1

Natural Farming

Grown without pesticides on our own land in Bobbili, Andhra Pradesh. The soil, the rain, the sun — nothing else.

2

Tree-Ripened

We let the fruit ripen on the tree until perfect — never picked early, never artificially ripened with carbide.

3

Hand-Picked

Every single mango is hand-plucked with care. No machines. No bruising. The love shows in every bite.

4

Hand-Graded

Each fruit graded by size (A+, A, B) and checked for ripeness. Only the best make it into your box.

5

Careful Packaging

Wrapped individually to prevent bruising. Packed in boxes that keep mangoes fresh through transit.

6

Delivered Fresh

Same-day delivery to Hyderabad. From Bobbili farm to your table — as fresh as it gets.
